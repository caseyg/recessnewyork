Title: levi's commuter pop up repair shop

----

Text: To promote the launch of the Levi's Commuter line for women , we produced a pop up bike repair shop in front of the Levi's stores in New York, Portland and Chicago, working with each city's respective local bike shops to offer free bike tune ups with any purchase of Levi's Commuter product the entire weekend of the launch.
